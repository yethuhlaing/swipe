# 2. Product Overview

SWIPE is a SaaS platform that sits on top of Instagram's official Graph API, Shopify, and WhatsApp Business API to turn a brand's existing DM-first wholesale workflow into a fully automated, CRM-backed pipeline. No no-code glue. No Airtable schemas to import. A brand connects their Instagram Business account, links their Shopify store, and is operational within 30 minutes.

---

## Core Principles

- **Instagram-native:** the buyer never leaves the platform they're already on
- **Zero commission:** flat SaaS fee regardless of GMV
- **Brand owns the data:** full export of buyer list, order history, conversations at any time
- **Human-in-the-loop:** AI assists but never fully removes the founder from high-value decisions
- **WhatsApp parity:** same pipeline works across IG DM and WhatsApp Business

---

## What SWIPE Is

| Layer | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Wholesale CRM** | Structured buyer database auto-populated from DM activity |
| **AI Inbox** | Unified IG DM + WhatsApp inbox with AI-drafted replies |
| **Pipeline Manager** | 7-stage Kanban tracking every buyer relationship |
| **Order Automation** | One-click Shopify draft order creation and invoice dispatch |
| **Reorder Engine** | Automated reorder outreach timed to each buyer's purchase cycle |
| **Analytics Layer** | Revenue, funnel, and buyer lifecycle reporting |

---

## What SWIPE Is Not

- **Not a consumer DM tool:** not for giveaways, comment-to-DM, or influencer flows — ManyChat owns that
- **Not a marketplace:** SWIPE doesn't bring buyers to you — it manages the buyers you already find
- **Not a Shopify replacement:** it extends Shopify's wholesale capabilities, not replaces them
- **Not an enterprise B2B tool:** not built for brands with dedicated sales teams (HubSpot + Shopify Plus serves them)

---

## Product Positioning in One Sentence

> SWIPE is to Instagram wholesale what Pipedrive is to B2B sales — a purpose-built pipeline tool that fits the workflow your team already uses, not one that forces you to change it.

---

## Onboarding Flow (Target: < 30 minutes to live)

```
1. Sign up → connect Instagram Business account via OAuth
2. Connect Shopify store via Shopify Partner OAuth
3. Import existing buyer list (CSV) or start fresh
4. Configure pipeline stage names (optional — defaults work)
5. Set wholesale price tier multipliers per buyer tier
6. Configure reorder timer defaults (default: 45 days)
7. Go live — SWIPE starts capturing new DM replies automatically
```
