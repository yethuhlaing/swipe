# 3. Target Customer

---

## Primary ICP — Independent Brand Founder

| Attribute | Profile |
|-----------|---------|
| **Industry** | Apparel, accessories, beauty, home goods, CPG |
| **Annual Wholesale Revenue** | $50K – $2M |
| **Team Size** | 1–5 people |
| **Current Tools** | Instagram DMs, Shopify Basic/Grow, Google Sheets, Brandboom |
| **Active Wholesale Buyers** | 10–200 retailer accounts |
| **Geography** | US, UK, Australia, Canada (English-first markets) |
| **Instagram Presence** | 5K – 200K followers, Business account |
| **Key Pain** | Wholesale takes 10–20 hrs/week of manual work, blocking growth |
| **Willingness to Pay** | $79–$199/mo — if it saves 5+ hours/week it pays for itself |

### Jobs-to-be-Done

- "I need to stop losing wholesale leads because I forgot to follow up"
- "I need to send invoices faster without building each draft order from scratch"
- "I need to know which buyers are worth my time before I invest hours in them"
- "I need my reorder rate to go up without me manually tracking who bought when"

---

## Secondary ICP — Wholesale Sales Rep / VA

Freelance sales reps or virtual assistants managing wholesale for 3–10 small brands. SWIPE's Agency tier lets them manage multiple brand accounts from a single dashboard, with per-brand billing they can pass through to clients.

| Attribute | Profile |
|-----------|---------|
| **Role** | Freelance wholesale rep, brand manager, or VA specializing in wholesale |
| **Brands Managed** | 3–10 simultaneously |
| **Current Pain** | Context-switching between different brand IG accounts, no unified view |
| **Willingness to Pay** | $299/mo per brand with volume discount — passes cost to client |

---

## Anti-ICP (Not Building For)

- **Enterprise brands** with dedicated B2B sales teams → HubSpot + Shopify Plus serves them
- **Marketplace-dependent brands** with no direct outreach strategy → needs Faire, not SWIPE
- **Brands with < 10 wholesale buyers** → no automation ROI yet; manual is fine
- **Brands not on Instagram** → wrong channel, product doesn't apply
- **Brands without Shopify** → v1 requires Shopify; future: WooCommerce support

---

## Customer Discovery Questions (for validation interviews)

1. How many hours per week do you currently spend on wholesale admin (DMs, invoicing, follow-ups)?
2. What does your current process look like from first DM to paid invoice?
3. What's your reorder rate, and how do you currently manage reorder outreach?
4. Have you tried any tools? What broke down?
5. If this tool saved you 8 hours a week, what would that be worth to you monthly?
