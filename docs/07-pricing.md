# 7. Pricing Model

Flat monthly SaaS. No commission on orders. No per-seat fees at Starter and Growth. Transparent and predictable for founders.

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Starter — $79/mo (or $69/mo billed annually)

**Best for:** Brands just starting wholesale, 10–50 active buyers

**Included:**
- Up to 50 active buyers in CRM
- 1 Instagram Business account
- Shopify product sync + one-click draft orders
- Basic Buyer CRM (auto-create, manual fields, tags, notes)
- AI-drafted DM replies (human-in-loop only — no auto-send)
- Core pipeline Kanban (7 stages)
- Invoice DM dispatch
- Payment detection webhook
- Basic analytics: pipeline funnel, GMV, avg order value, reorder rate
- Reorder timer (manual trigger only)
- CSV export
- Email support

---

### Growth — $199/mo (or $169/mo billed annually)

**Best for:** Brands with 50–200 active buyers doing $100K–$1M wholesale

**Everything in Starter, plus:**
- Up to 300 active buyers
- IG + WhatsApp Business in unified inbox
- Full AI auto-responder with confidence threshold setting
- Buyer qualification scoring (1–10 AI score)
- Product card DM generation (in-thread image cards)
- Starter pack builder + AI product recommendations
- Net terms tracking (Net 15/30/60) + overdue alerts
- Automated reorder sequences (multi-touch cadence)
- Order status DMs (shipped + delivered notifications)
- Seasonal reorder overrides
- Slack integration (human handoff alerts + weekly digest)
- Klaviyo buyer list sync
- Bulk buyer import via CSV
- Hot lead alerts
- AI performance analytics
- Response time metrics
- Priority support (< 4hr response)

---

### Scale — $499/mo (or $419/mo billed annually)

**Best for:** Brands doing $500K+ wholesale, multiple accounts, advanced needs

**Everything in Growth, plus:**
- Unlimited active buyers
- Up to 3 Instagram accounts
- Brand voice AI training (per-tenant fine-tuning)
- Reorder one-click confirmation ('yes' reply → auto-draft order)
- Competitor carry detection
- Territory map (geographic buyer distribution)
- Lead score decay system
- Cohort analysis + retention reporting
- Multi-currency invoicing (via Shopify Markets)
- Partial payment tracking
- Password-protected buyer-specific catalog
- Faire Direct buyer import
- Custom domain for digital catalog
- Zapier / Make webhook output
- Shopify B2B (Plus) integration
- Dedicated onboarding call + account manager
- SLA: 99.9% uptime guarantee

---

### Agency — $299/mo per brand (volume discounts available)

**Best for:** Freelance wholesale reps and VAs managing 3–10 brands

**Included:**
- All Growth features per brand
- Unified multi-brand dashboard with brand switcher
- Separate login credentials per brand (brand-level access control)
- Consolidated billing with per-brand line items
- White-label option (remove SWIPE branding from buyer-facing catalog pages)
- Volume discount: 10% off 3–5 brands, 20% off 6–10 brands
- Affiliate program: 20% rev-share for referred brands (12-month duration)

---

## Pricing Rationale vs. Alternatives

| Alternative | Cost | SWIPE Advantage |
|-------------|------|----------------|
| **Faire** | 15–25% commission | At $10K/mo GMV, Faire costs $1,500–2,500/mo. SWIPE Growth = $199/mo. Brand also keeps buyer list. |
| **Shopify B2B** | $2,000+/mo (Plus plan) | SWIPE is 10x cheaper and IG-native. No catalog migration required. |
| **ManyChat Pro (25K contacts)** | $260/mo | Zero wholesale features: no CRM, no invoicing, no reorder engine, no Shopify B2B integration. |
| **Make.com + Airtable DIY** | $150–400/mo + 40–80hrs setup | SWIPE: 30-min onboarding, always maintained, no Make.com breakage on API changes. |
| **HubSpot Sales Pro** | $800+/mo | No IG DM native integration, massive overkill for 1–5 person teams. |

## Unit Economics (Estimates)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| CAC (paid) | < $200 |
| CAC (content/community) | < $50 |
| Avg contract value (ACV) | ~$2,400/yr (blended) |
| Gross margin | ~75% (AI API costs are primary COGS) |
| Target payback period | < 3 months |
| LTV (at 24mo avg retention) | ~$4,800 |
| LTV:CAC ratio | > 10:1 at scale |

---

## Billing Notes

- All plans billed monthly or annually (annual = 2 months free equivalent)
- 14-day free trial on Starter and Growth — no credit card required
- Downgrade/upgrade mid-cycle: prorated
- Cancellation: immediate, data retained 90 days then deleted (GDPR option: immediate deletion)
- Enterprise / custom pricing available for brands with > 500 buyers or multi-territory needs
