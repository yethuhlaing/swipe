# 11. Competitive Positioning

---

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Category | Their Gap | SWIPE Advantage |
|------------|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Faire** | B2B marketplace (discovery + fulfillment) | 15–25% commission, owns buyer list, no DM automation, forces catalog migration | Zero commission, brand owns buyers, IG-native, no migration required. Complements Faire (Faire Direct import) — doesn't replace it. |
| **ManyChat** | Consumer DM automation | No CRM, no wholesale pipeline, no invoicing, no Shopify B2B integration, built for consumer giveaways | Purpose-built for B2B wholesale workflow. Completely different buyer intent. |
| **Shopify B2B** | Enterprise wholesale storefront | $2,000+/mo (Plus plan), requires full catalog migration, no IG-native flow, complex onboarding | 10x cheaper at Growth tier, works within existing IG workflow, integrates with Shopify instead of competing. |
| **Make.com + Airtable (DIY)** | No-code automation stack | 40–80 hrs setup, breaks on Meta API changes, no product support, requires technical skill | Managed SaaS: 30-min onboarding, always updated, no technical setup, supported. |
| **HubSpot** | Enterprise CRM | $800+/mo, no IG DM native integration, massive overkill for 1–5 person teams | Right-sized for indie brand teams, IG-native, 10x cheaper. |
| **Brandboom** | Wholesale catalog tool | No CRM, no DM automation, no reorder engine, just a catalog/linebook tool | SWIPE includes catalog as one feature within a full pipeline system. Can replace Brandboom dependency. |
| **Orderchamp / Ankorstore** | European B2B marketplace | Marketplace model: same commission problem as Faire, discovery-focused | SWIPE: direct brand-to-buyer relationships, zero commission, IG-native. |

## Key Differentiators (Sales Talking Points)

### vs. Faire
> "Faire is great for discovery — finding new buyers. SWIPE is for managing those buyers once you have them, without paying 15-25% commission forever. Most brands should use both."

### vs. ManyChat
> "ManyChat is for getting consumer followers to click a link. SWIPE is a B2B CRM — completely different workflow, completely different buyer. SWIPE is what you use after ManyChat generates interest."

### vs. Shopify B2B / Plus
> "Shopify B2B is powerful but requires a $2,000/mo platform upgrade and expects buyers to log into a portal. SWIPE meets buyers where they already are — in your Instagram DMs — and requires no buyer behavior change."

### vs. DIY stack (Make + Airtable)
> "The DIY stack works until Meta changes their API, which they do regularly. Then your automations break, and you're spending a weekend fixing it. SWIPE maintains the infrastructure — your only job is to approve or send messages."
