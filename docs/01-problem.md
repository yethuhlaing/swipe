# 1. The Problem

Thousands of small apparel, beauty, and CPG brands run their entire wholesale operation through Instagram DMs. A founder or VA manually messages boutique buyers, shares a Brandboom or Google Drive catalog link, follows up over days, builds a draft order in Shopify, chases payment, and manually remembers to send a reorder message 45 days later. Every step is ad-hoc, undocumented, and unscalable.

---

## Current Pain Points

- **No CRM:** buyer conversations live in Instagram DM threads, unsearchable and untracked
- **Manual invoicing:** draft orders built from scratch per buyer, each taking 20–40 minutes
- **Zero follow-up system:** reorder reminders forgotten or inconsistent
- **Qualification guesswork:** no signal on whether a buyer is serious before investing time
- **Expensive alternatives:** Faire charges 15–25% commission and owns your buyer relationships
- **Complex alternatives:** Shopify B2B requires Plus ($2,000+/mo), full catalog migration, and retailer onboarding

---

## Market Context

> **Key data points:**
> - Global B2B wholesale GMV projected at **$25 trillion** in 2025 (Grand View Research)
> - Gartner: **65%** of B2B buyers complete higher-quality deals when self-navigating vs. 24% through reps
> - Faire charges **25% commission** on opening orders and **15% on reorders** — SWIPE's SaaS model has zero commission
> - Faire's 800K+ retailer network creates discovery value, but brands **lose customer relationships entirely**
> - McKinsey: data-driven teams blending personalized CX with GenAI are **1.7x more likely** to expand market share

---

## The Core Workflow Being Replaced

```
Founder finds buyer on IG
  → Sends cold DM manually
    → Buyer replies → Founder pastes Brandboom link
      → Follows up 2–3 days later via DM
        → Buyer asks for recommendation → Founder manually suggests products
          → Builds draft order in Shopify (20–40 min)
            → Sends invoice link in DM
              → Waits for payment → Checks Shopify daily
                → 45 days later: tries to remember to follow up for reorder
```

Every arrow is a manual, context-switching step. SWIPE automates or assists every step after the initial cold DM.
