# 10. Go-To-Market Strategy

---

## GTM Principle

SWIPE's customers live on Instagram. They talk about business problems in Facebook groups, Reddit, and niche Slack communities. They trust peer recommendations over ads. The GTM strategy prioritizes **community-led and content-led growth** over paid acquisition — lower CAC, higher trust, and the product is literally about IG which makes content creation authentic.

---

## Channel 1: Instagram-Native Content

**Owner:** Founder  
**Timeline:** Start immediately, before MVP launch

SWIPE's brand account documents wholesale automation wins in real time. The content strategy:

- **"Before/After" posts:** show manual DM-to-invoice process vs. SWIPE-automated version
- **Founder POV content:** real stories of brands saving 10+ hours/week
- **Tutorial Reels:** "How I automated my wholesale reorders in 30 minutes"
- **Results screenshots:** with permission, share metrics from beta users

**Distribution tactic:** Use ManyChat comment-to-DM (ironic but effective) to capture leads from posts. Anyone who comments "DM" or "how?" on a post gets an automated intro + trial link sent to their inbox.

**Target:** 5–10 qualified inbound leads per week by month 6

---

## Channel 2: Wholesale Founder Communities

**Owner:** Founder  
**Timeline:** Active from Phase 0

High-signal communities where the ICP spends time and asks exactly the problems SWIPE solves:

| Community | Platform | Size | Approach |
|-----------|----------|------|----------|
| Faire seller groups | Facebook | 50K+ members | Genuine help posts, no sales pitches |
| Shopify wholesale threads | Reddit r/shopify | Active daily | Answer questions, link to SWIPE when relevant |
| Independent boutique owner groups | Facebook | 100K+ members | Same — help first |
| Apparel / fashion brand founder Slack groups | Slack | Varies | Introduce product authentically |
| Wholesale by Wanderer (email newsletter) | Email | ~20K readers | Sponsored slot or guest post |

**Tactic:** Spend 30 min/day genuinely answering questions about wholesale workflow. Build reputation as an expert. Mention SWIPE when it naturally solves the problem being discussed. Do not post "check out my tool" — contribute value first.

---

## Channel 3: VA and Wholesale Rep Network

**Owner:** Growth (Month 6+)  
**Timeline:** Activate with Agency tier launch

Freelance VAs and wholesale reps who manage IG for brands are a **multiplier channel**: each rep adopts SWIPE for their workflow and brings 3–10 client brands with them.

**How to activate:**
- Join VA networks and freelancer communities (Fancy Hands, VA Facebook groups, Upwork wholesale niche)
- Offer Agency tier with 20% rev-share affiliate on all referred brands (12-month duration)
- Create "VA Certification" program: free training + SWIPE-certified badge they can use in their profiles
- Provide co-branded materials: VA can offer "SWIPE-powered wholesale management" as a service

**Target:** 20 VA affiliates each bringing 2–3 brands = 40–60 additional customers

---

## Channel 4: Shopify App Store

**Owner:** Development team  
**Timeline:** Phase 3 (Month 10–12)

Shopify App Store listing provides:
- Organic discovery from brands searching wholesale tools
- Trust signal (Shopify reviews)
- Distribution to Shopify Plus brands for Scale tier upsell

**App Store positioning:**
- Primary category: Sales channels → Wholesale
- Headline: "Instagram DM Wholesale CRM — From cold DM to paid invoice, automated"
- Target 4.5+ star rating with 50+ reviews before promoting heavily

## Launch Sequence

### Week 1 (MVP ready)
- Email waitlist built during Phase 0 (target: 200 brands)
- Offer first 50 brands 3 months free (Starter) in exchange for weekly feedback calls
- Post launch thread in top 3 wholesale communities

### Month 1 post-launch
- Collect 3–5 detailed case studies from beta users (specific hours saved, GMV increase)
- Publish case studies as long-form IG carousel posts and website pages
- Start weekly email newsletter: "The Wholesale OS" — tips for IG-first brands

### Ongoing
- Weekly community posts (help-first)
- Bi-weekly product update emails to customer list
- Monthly webinar: "Automate Your Wholesale Pipeline in 60 Minutes"
